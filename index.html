Two Approaches

01
Professional

Operational Documents

The operational documents are built for people who work with these forms. They explain what the forms require, how to fill them out, and what happens if you get it wrong. No mysticism — just the rules, the procedures, and the consequences of ignoring them.

02
Theological

Symbolic Reading

The biblical exegesis steps back and reads the same material through a different framework. It treats the text as something that might carry meaning beyond its stated purpose — not because it does, but because the exercise of reading it that way produces something worth reading.

How to Use This Index

If you are preparing a DTC eligibility filing, processing a trade payment, or completing an SF 28, use the operational documents. They contain the information you need. If you are interested in reading financial documents as religious texts, use the exegesis. It will not help you file anything, but it may give you something to think about. The two are not substitutes for each other.
